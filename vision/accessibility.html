Accessibility commitment.

Vision Studio Lab aims to maintain readable typography, keyboard accessibility, and controlled contrast across public pages and the Studio shell.

Current focus

Readable dark surfaces, keyboard focus states, and clear interaction hierarchy across public pages and Studio access flows.

Need help?

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, contact hello@visionstudiolab.com and describe the issue.

Response target

We aim to review reported accessibility issues promptly and prioritize fixes that block access or comprehension.
